About OpenSensorCloud

OpenSensorCloud enable your internet of things project, by collecting informations from your devices, and make sense and provides actionnable

Small or Big projects

We are big support of creativity and we support small developer projects as well as big ones

Customizable

OpenSensorCloud is higly customizable, and you can create in minutes dashboards displaying your data, and share this informations with others

Standard Based

OpenSensorCloud use standard protocols, like Rest, and MQTT and is commited to support more emerging IOT standard

Simple to use

You can use it out of the box, and start in a minute to capture data generated by your device. You can easily create graph, dashboard, triggers on these datas
